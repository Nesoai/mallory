A corner of the neighborhood, since 2019.

Marigold & Oak started with one oven, a borrowed espresso machine, and a simple idea: a place where neighbors could slow down for a minute. We still bake everything by hand, still know most orders by heart, and still save the corner table for whoever needs it most.
