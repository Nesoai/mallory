Freelancer work tracker · live example

Tracking freelance work across platforms

I started this in late June 2026 to answer a practical question: which procurement and contract platforms are actually worth a freelancer’s time? It records every proposal in one place — the role, the source, the status, the outcome — so patterns surface: where the best-fit, best-paid work comes from, and which sites aren’t worth it. It’s a live example of how any freelancer or small team can monitor their pipeline across sources — and of the kind of interactive dashboard I build for clients. Explore it; it’s fully live.
