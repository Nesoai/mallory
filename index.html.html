04 · The approach

AI and human — not one instead of the other.

The best work today isn't all-human, all-AI, or a person rubber-stamping whatever a model produced. It's a real collaboration: AI to move fast across information, a human to think, question, and decide.

I build for people who want to think for themselves — to weigh well-rounded information and choose deliberately, instead of regurgitating the last video, podcast, or opinion they scrolled past. Better inputs, real judgment, boundless outcomes.
